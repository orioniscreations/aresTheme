Ba'ul has been Ares' live long friend. He was the one who found Ares as an infant, and took him to the Rovers for them to look after, and hasn't left his side for long since.

He's caring, patient, and laid-back. Like most Absol, Ba'ul dislikes fighting, and prefers to enjoy a carefree lifestyle, where all that is ever expected of him is to occasionally predict disasters, or allow his 'Family' to use him as an attraction in shows every now and then.

Most of the time though, he's usually following someone around, and trying to beg for snacks. There is little that Ba'ul seems to care about outside of food, and is not above begging for treats.

What he was doing in the Cursed Ruins, how he found Ares, and why he brought him to that peticular Rover Kumpania, are all secrets he keeps to himself.
